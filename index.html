A data-driven analysis of Stronghold's digital presence, supporter behavior, and growth opportunities based on:

The Big Picture

Stronghold has built something exceptional: a mission that saves lives, a supporter base that believes deeply, and world-class loyalty metrics. The challenge is structural, not motivational. The systems needed to grow are missing or broken.

The Foundation is Strong

  • NPS of 85.6 (world-class)
  • 89% of supporters would recommend you
  • 97.4% had a positive donation experience
  • The core is exceptional

The Infrastructure is Not

  • Churn is outpacing acquisition
  • Search visibility is near zero
  • Failed payments costing hundreds of thousands annually
  • The delivery system is severely underperforming
