A data-driven analysis of Stronghold's digital presence, donor behavior, and growth opportunities based on 441,000+ donation records and 341 supporter responses.

The Big Picture

Stronghold has built something exceptional: a mission that saves lives, a supporter base that believes deeply, and world-class loyalty metrics. The challenge is structural, not motivational. The systems needed to grow are missing or broken.

The Foundation is Strong

NPS of 85.6 (world-class), 89% of supporters would recommend you, 97.4% had a positive donation experience. The core is exceptional.

The Infrastructure is Not

Churn is outpacing acquisition. Search visibility is near zero. Failed payments are costing hundreds of thousands annually. The delivery system is severely underperforming.
