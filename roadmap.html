1

Stop the Bleeding

Immediate Priority | Revenue Recovery Focus

Why this first: Before building anything new, we stop the active losses. These are quick wins that recover revenue from your existing supporter base with minimal effort.

Failed Payment Recovery

Activate FundraiseUp's Card Account Updater ($0.25/card). Automatically updates saved payment methods when cards are replaced.

$100K+ Annual Recovery

Cancellation Triage

Cross-reference cancellation signals with donor value. High-value supporters who cancel trigger personal outreach from leadership.

Retention Priority

Re-engagement Flows

"Sorry to see you go" email sequence offering lower tiers or pause options instead of full cancellation.

Churn Prevention

Milestone Recognition

Recognition at key thresholds: 1-year postcard, 2-year thank you. Supporters past 10 months are much more likely to stay.

Loyalty Building

Direct Mail Outreach

"Brief a Friend" postcard packs and "Cover Your 6" referral cards. Physical touchpoints that supporters can share with their network.

Word of Mouth

Fix GA4 / FundraiseUp Integration

Critical infrastructure: map FundraiseUp donation events to GA4. Without this, we cannot measure whether Phase 1 actions are working.

Measurement Prerequisite

2

Website Rebuild

Foundation Work | Platform Migration

Why this second: Move off Squarespace to a proper CMS. Nothing else scales until this is done. The website is the foundation for everything that follows.

Platform Migration

Move to a CMS that supports proper analytics, content management, and conversion optimization. Squarespace is a ceiling on growth.

Critical

Conversion Infrastructure

Fix GA4 event mapping. Implement UTM tracking on all links. FundraiseUp captures UTMs natively. Enable proper attribution.

Measurement Fix

Stories Hub

Consolidate thin /updates/ posts into evergreen regional authority pages. Structure content for SEO and engagement.

Content Architecture

YouTube Landing Page

Dedicated landing page for video visitors with video-specific CTAs and conversion path. Captures traffic from MrBallen and other video sources.

Conversion Path

Impact Transparency

Show exactly how funds are used. Add impact equivalencies: "$50/month = 3 med packs in the field."

Trust Building

Social Proof

Donors need to feel they are joining a movement. "Join 10,300 people supporting rescue operations."

Conversion Lift

3

Growth Channels

Acquisition Engine | Paid + Organic

Why this third: With the foundation fixed, activate the channels that will drive new donor acquisition. Free channels first, then paid amplification.

Google Ad Grants

$10,000/month in free search ads for nonprofits. Most organizations don't know this exists. Stronghold likely already qualifies. Requires conversion tracking to be fixed first.

$120K/Year Free Media

Email Strategy

Email is the #1 retention channel. Most current donors came through podcasts, not social media. Build quality sequences.

Retention Focus

Social Media Paid

Boost field dispatches within 24 hours of posting. $50-100 targeting US adults 25-65 interested in humanitarian aid and veterans.

Amplification

SEO Content

Build intent-mapped pages targeting non-branded searches: "veteran charity," "Burma relief," "humanitarian evacuation."

Long-Term Growth

YouTube / Podcast Content

Create Stronghold's own content series. Video dispatches, operator interviews, mission debriefs. Build owned audience on YouTube.

Content Engine

Referral Program

89% of supporters would recommend Stronghold. Give them a structured way to refer. "Cover Your 6" postcard packs.

Word of Mouth

Communication Strategy

Structured engagement across channels. For example:

  • Monthly "State of the Union" video updates
  • Stronghold podcast series
  • YouTube Live for high-value donors

Scale

Paid Acquisition | Data-Driven Optimization

Why this last: With audience intelligence from Phase 3, invest in paid search when you know who converts. Expand partnerships. Build the ambassador program.

Google PPC

Layer in paid search spend in Q4 when you have the audience intelligence to target efficiently. Year-end giving season.

Strategic Investment

Podcast Partnerships

47% of donors came from MrBallen. This is a single point of failure. Model new partnerships on that playbook.

Diversification

Ambassador Program

73 "super fans" have made 49+ donations each. Give them a formal role. Brand ambassadors, event hosts, referral champions.

Community Building

Major Donor Cultivation

One-time donors give 9.6x more per transaction. Build a separate funnel for major donation prospects. Personal cultivation.
