Stop the Bleeding

Immediate Priority | Revenue Recovery Focus

Why this first: Before building anything new, we stop the active losses. These are quick wins that recover revenue from your existing supporter base with minimal effort.

Failed Payment Recovery

Activate FundraiseUp's Card Account Updater ($0.25/card). Automatically updates saved payment methods when cards are replaced.

$100K+ Annual Recovery

Cancellation Triage

Cross-reference cancellation signals with donor value. High-value supporters who cancel trigger personal outreach from leadership.

Retention Priority

Re-engagement Flows

"Sorry to see you go" email sequence offering lower tiers or pause options instead of full cancellation.

Churn Prevention

Milestone Recognition

Recognition at key thresholds: 1-year postcard, 2-year thank you. Supporters past 10 months are much more likely to stay.

Loyalty Building
2

Website Rebuild

Foundation Work | Platform Migration

Why this second: Move off Squarespace to a proper CMS. Nothing else scales until this is done. The website is the foundation for everything that follows.

Platform Migration

Move to a CMS that supports proper analytics, content management, and conversion optimization. Squarespace is a ceiling on growth.

Critical

Conversion Infrastructure

Fix GA4 event mapping. Implement UTM tracking on all links. FundraiseUp captures UTMs natively. Enable proper attribution.

Measurement Fix

Stories Hub

Consolidate thin /updates/ posts into evergreen regional authority pages. Structure content for SEO and engagement.

Content Architecture

YouTube Landing Page

"You just watched this. Here's how to help." Dedicated page for video-referred visitors. Unlock 34,000 monthly YouTube visitors.

Conversion Path

Impact Transparency

Show exactly how funds are used. Add impact equivalencies: "$50/month = 3 med packs in the field."

Trust Building

Social Proof

"Join 10,300 people supporting rescue operations." Donors need to feel they are joining a movement.

Conversion Lift
3

Growth Channels

Acquisition Engine | Paid + Organic

Why this third: With the foundation fixed, activate the channels that will drive new donor acquisition. Free channels first, then paid amplification.

Google Ad Grants

$10,000/month in free search ads for nonprofits. Requires conversion tracking to be fixed first. Captures high-intent searches.

$120K/Year Free Media

Email Strategy

Email is the #1 retention channel. Most current donors came through podcasts, not social media. Build quality sequences.

Retention Focus

Social Media Paid

Boost field dispatches within 24 hours of posting. $50-100 targeting US adults 25-65 interested in humanitarian aid and veterans.

Amplification

SEO Content

Build intent-mapped pages targeting non-branded searches: "veteran charity," "Burma relief," "humanitarian evacuation."

Long-Term Growth

Referral Program

89% of supporters would recommend Stronghold. Give them a structured way to refer. "Cover Your 6" postcard packs.

Word of Mouth

Monthly State of the Union

Scripted monthly video update on what's happening. Feed through YouTube. Maintain engagement and provide shareable content.
